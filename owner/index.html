Scotty Shaw (Duke University, Class of 2009) serves as the European Commission's #EUvsVirus CTO and National Curator of the United States.


His journey as a hackathon expert started in 2014 with HackDuke: Code For Good, the premier hackathon for social impact and Duke University's official hackathon.


Since January 2017, he has been the Founder/CEO/President of Tech Tree Root, an #EUvsVirus Initiator and the national hackathon organization of the United States.


Connect with Scotty Shaw online  >
Tech Tree Root is a registered Public Charity Organization of the United States and creates innovation ecosystems by generating opportunities and resources for technological and entrepreneurial education.

501c3 Educational Nonprofit Tax ID: 83-3634028

Before the American coronavirus national emergency declaration, Tech Tree Root launched HackCOVID, the national anti-coronavirus hackathon of the United States.
